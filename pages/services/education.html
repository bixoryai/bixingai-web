Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our AI education programs

Who should attend your AI training programs?

Our programs are designed for various roles and experience levels. The AI Fundamentals program is ideal for non-technical professionals who need to understand AI concepts. The Applied AI program is best for technical team members who will be directly involved in implementing AI solutions. The AI Leadership program is tailored for executives and decision-makers responsible for AI strategy.

Do participants need prior programming experience?

For the AI Fundamentals program, no programming experience is required. For the Applied AI program, basic programming knowledge (preferably Python) is recommended. We can provide pre-training resources to help participants prepare if needed.

Can you customize the training for our specific industry?

Yes, we specialize in industry-specific customization. Our training programs can be tailored to address the unique AI applications, challenges, and opportunities in your sector, whether it's healthcare, finance, manufacturing, retail, or any other industry.

Do you offer virtual training options?

Yes, all our training programs are available in both in-person and virtual formats. Our virtual training maintains the same interactive, hands-on approach as our in-person sessions, with additional digital collaboration tools to ensure an engaging learning experience.

What ongoing support do you provide after the training?

All participants receive access to our online learning portal with additional resources, practice exercises, and reference materials for 6 months after the training. We also offer optional follow-up consulting sessions to help teams apply their new knowledge to specific projects.
