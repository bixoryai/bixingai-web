Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our AI education programs

Who should attend your AI training programs?

Our programs are designed for various audiences, from technical teams (data scientists, engineers, developers) to business professionals (managers, analysts, executives) who need to understand and leverage AI. We tailor the content to match the participants' roles and existing knowledge.

How long do your training programs typically last?

Program duration varies based on depth and scope. We offer everything from executive briefings (half-day), foundational workshops (2-3 days), to comprehensive bootcamps (1-4 weeks). We can also design extended learning journeys with spaced sessions over several months.

Do you offer virtual training options?

Yes, all our programs are available in-person, virtual, or hybrid formats. Our virtual training maintains the interactive and hands-on nature of our in-person sessions through collaborative tools and virtual lab environments.

How do you customize programs for our specific industry?

We begin with a thorough assessment of your industry context, challenges, and goals. Our curriculum designers then adapt our core content with industry-specific examples, case studies, and exercises. We can also incorporate your organization's own data and use cases when appropriate.
